Why The Space Index?

If you are a new to space and want to learn more, it could be compicated to search for inforamtion and learn.

There tons of people in the internet who hates a particular organisation and make videos hating on them.

Here at The Space Index, we believe that every organisation should be appereciated because they (not all) benefits everyone in science, exploration and other fields.

The Space Index is created to help new hobbyist or people who want to learn more about space to find useful inforamtion by compiling them together, and adding extra information and tools.
